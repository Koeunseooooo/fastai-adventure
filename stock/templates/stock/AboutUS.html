We are STOCKer

To provide the most accurate stock information


Many people around the world use the stock market. Someone for fun, some for making a lot of money, some for a job. One day, we
wondered whether we could provide better services to these users. So, we decided to use auto TA technology to predict the future
stock graph and notify our users through an alarm.


STOCKer wants to solve the biggest problems in the stock market. We are 8 smart and cool computer science students. We're developers,
desiners, and data scientists. Corona-19 prevented us from working together in certain spaces, but we were able to work efficiently using Zoom or Google Meeting.


FASTOCK was founded in 2021 by CAU univ and Peoplespace INC. It started with computer science students sharing research and
ideas. First, we started with an analysis of the stock market first, then we identified market trends to understand what stock market users needed, and we developed a program tailored to their needs.


FASTOCK currently boasts 96 percent accuracy, which is among the best in the industry. We plan to further expand this business in
connection with numerous global fund management and stock markets in the future.
